About the Artist

I Capture
Moments of Grace

✦

Hello — I'm Sophia Claire, a fine-art bridal photographer based in New York, travelling worldwide to document love stories. With over a decade behind the lens, I believe every wedding has its own quiet poetry waiting to be seen.

My approach is gentle and unobtrusive. I blend into your day so the moments I capture feel completely, beautifully real — not posed, not performed. Just you, as you truly are.

— Sophia Claire
